Rice Bowl

Authentic Chinese Cuisine in the Heart of Dubai

Enjoy a True Asian Experience

Welcome to Dubai's premiere fusion of Chinese and Japanese Cuisine. Dine in one of our themed Chinese rooms, private Japanese suites, or the new sushi bar. Three separate kitchens manage a full menu of authentic dishes.

"Food is our common ground, a universal experience."

- Howard Fong

A family business with a rich history...

Rice Bowl was established by Howard and Jade Fong in 1959. Since then, the restaurant and prospered and expanded. The Chef's Special Honey Walnut Shrimp has been a mainstay and remains one of Dubai's best-loved dishes.

In 1999, the Fongs decided to retire. Their children took it over, renovated the entire building and established 3 different cuisines (Chinese, Japanese and Sushi).

And even though Howard and Jade Fong are "retired," they visit the restaurant often. They can be seen here daily, greeting new and old guests and upholding the spirit of Asian hospitality.
